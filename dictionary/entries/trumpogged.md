# trumpogged

v. The compound defeat with ontological damage. To be simultaneously outdone and dominated — but the peanemus is what gets caught between them. Your very essence is the connective tissue between two forms of defeat. The "o" in trump-o-gged IS the peanemus — the irreducible syllable reduced to a single vowel, the nut of being crushed between trump and mog.
