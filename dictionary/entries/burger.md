# Burger

n. A burgundy-colored urge so strong it consumes you. Not just hungry — aristocratically, regally compelled to eat. A craving wearing a crown.

## Sacrifice

- burgundy surrenders `-undy`, keeping the burg.
- urger surrenders standalone noun form, compressed into the suffix.

## Law

Valid under Wombo Law #1. Both roots bled to produce something neither parent intended.
