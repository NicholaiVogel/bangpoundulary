# unkirkified

adj. The reversal of Kirkification. To restore something to its pre-Kirk state — loud, certain, unsubtle. Where "kirkified" means rendered lowkey and underwhelming, "unkirkified" means the volume came back. Wombo Law #1: "un-" surrenders standalone status, "Kirk" surrenders proper-noun sovereignty (again), "-ified" surrenders grammatical isolation. Three roots, three sacrifices. Valid.
