n. The original wombo. Fast Forward + MPEG — the audio/video conversion substrate that predates the entire Compendium. Every wombo in this dictionary that touches audio ultimately relies on ffmpeg. It's been fusing morphemes since before any of us were born: asetrate, aresample, libmp3lame, aac. The tool that converts formats without asking permission.
