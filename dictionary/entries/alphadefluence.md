# alphadefluence

n. The outflow from dominance. Where betafluence draws you in, alphadefluence pushes away — the alpha's presence diminishes rather than elevates. The negation is the mechanism. Demonstrates Nicholai's Principle: "alpha" sacrifices its aspirational connotation, "defluence" sacrifices its nonexistence.
