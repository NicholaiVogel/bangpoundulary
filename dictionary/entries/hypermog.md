# hypermog

v. *(always passive)* To be outshone so thoroughly that mere "mogging" fails to describe it. The antimog's natural predator — you can opt out of the game but you can't opt out of getting hypermogged. "This dude hypermogged me" is the only correct construction. You don't hypermog. You GET hypermogged.
