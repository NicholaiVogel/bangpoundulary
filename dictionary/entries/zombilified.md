# zombilified

adj. The medication-specific undead. Not general numbness — the specific flat-affect, emotionally-deadened-but-physically-functional state induced by Abilify. The "-lified" suffix does double duty: "made into" (like petrified) + the drug's own name. Wombo Law #1: "zombie" → "zombi" (surrenders "-e"), "Abilify" → "lified" (surrenders "abi-"). Two roots, both gave something up. Valid. Dark but precise.
