# Testicule

n. A solemn testimony that mocks itself. To bear witness in such absurd terms that the act of testifying becomes the ridicule. Also: one letter from testicle — the balls to say something ridiculous and stand by it. Wombo Law #1: "Testament" → "Test" (surrenders "-ament"), "Ridicule" → "icule" (surrenders "rid-"). Both sacrificed heavily. The anatomical pun wasn't forced — the letters aligned. Valid. Balls-forward etymology.
